<p><ul><li>Physical Assay</li><li>Chemical Assay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable water analysis instrumentation market comprises two primary types: physical assays and chemical assays. Physical assays focus on measuring parameters like turbidity, temperature, and conductivity, providing real-time information on water quality. In contrast, chemical assays analyze specific substances or contaminants in water, such as pH, dissolved oxygen, and harmful chemicals, often requiring more complex instrumentation. Both types play a crucial role in environmental monitoring, ensuring safe drinking water, and managing aquatic ecosystems effectively.</p></p>
