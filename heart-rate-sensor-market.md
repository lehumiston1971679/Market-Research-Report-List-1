<p><p>The heart rate sensor market is primarily divided into two types: photoplethysmography (PPG) sensors and electrocardiography (ECG) sensors. PPG sensors use light absorption techniques to measure blood volume changes, offering non-invasive heart rate monitoring, primarily in wearables like fitness trackers. In contrast, ECG sensors capture electrical signals generated by the heart, providing accurate and detailed heart activity insights, commonly used in medical devices. Both technologies cater to different applications, from consumer fitness to clinical diagnostics, enhancing health monitoring capabilities.</p></p>
