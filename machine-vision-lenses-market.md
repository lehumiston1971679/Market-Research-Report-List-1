<p><p>Machine vision lenses are essential components in imaging systems used for automated inspection and analysis. C-Mount lenses are widely used for their versatility and standardization. CS-Mount lenses are more compact, offering a shorter flange distance. F-Mount lenses, primarily used in still photography, can also be adapted for machine vision. V-Mount lenses provide a robust solution for video applications. T2-Mount lenses are adaptable for various imaging needs. Other market segments include specialty mounts catering to specific industrial or scientific applications.</p></p>
