<p><strong>The Smart Machine Vision System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Line Scan Camera System</li><li>Area Scan Camera System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart Machine Vision System market comprises two primary types: Line Scan Camera Systems and Area Scan Camera Systems. Line Scan Cameras capture images one line at a time, making them ideal for applications involving continuous movement, such as conveyor belts. This allows for high-speed imaging of long objects. Conversely, Area Scan Cameras capture an entire frame in a single shot, making them suitable for capturing static scenes or large objects. Both systems enhance automation and quality control in various industrial applications.</p></p>
