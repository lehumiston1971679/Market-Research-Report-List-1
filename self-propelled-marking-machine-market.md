<p><p>The self-propelled marking machine market is divided into two primary types: self-propelled thermoplastic road marking machines and self-propelled cold paint road marking machines. Thermoplastic machines utilize heated materials that adhere to surfaces, offering durability and visibility, ideal for high-traffic areas. In contrast, cold paint machines apply liquid paint that dries quickly, making them suitable for temporary markings and situations requiring quick turnaround. Both types cater to varying road marking needs based on performance, longevity, and application methods.</p></p>
