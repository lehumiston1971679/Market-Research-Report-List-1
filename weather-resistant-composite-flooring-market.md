<p><p>Weather-resistant composite flooring is categorized into two main types: three-layer and multilayer systems. Three-layer flooring typically consists of a top protective layer, a core layer for stability, and a backing layer for moisture resistance. Multilayer systems involve multiple layers, combining various materials for enhanced durability, aesthetic appeal, and better resistance to environmental factors. Both types cater to outdoor applications, offering resilience against harsh weather conditions, while ensuring versatility in design and installation across residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
