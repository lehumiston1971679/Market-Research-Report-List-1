<p><strong>The Wideband Couplers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wideband Directional Coupler</li><li>Wideband Hybrid Coupler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wideband couplers market primarily features two types: wideband directional couplers and wideband hybrid couplers. Wideband directional couplers are designed to measure and split signals efficiently while maintaining isolation between ports, making them ideal for RF applications. On the other hand, wideband hybrid couplers combine input signals from two different sources, evenly distributing power to outputs. Both types are essential in telecommunications, broadcasting, and various electronic systems, ensuring optimal signal integrity and functionality across a wide frequency range.</p></p>
