<p><p>The DBC (Direct Bonded Copper) ceramic substrate market primarily consists of two key types: AlN (Aluminum Nitride) and Al2O3 (Alumina) DBC ceramic substrates. AlN substrates are favored for high thermal conductivity and electrical insulation, making them ideal for high-power applications. In contrast, Al2O3 substrates, known for their affordability and good electrical insulation properties, are widely used in various electronic devices. Both types cater to different industry needs, ranging from automotive to telecommunications, enhancing performance and reliability.</p></p>
