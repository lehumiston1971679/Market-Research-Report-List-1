<p><ul><li>Air-Operated Diaphragm Pump</li><li>Electrically Operated Diaphragm Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diaphragm dry pump market includes two primary types: air-operated diaphragm pumps and electrically operated diaphragm pumps. Air-operated diaphragm pumps use compressed air to create a vacuum and move fluid, making them suitable for various industrial applications. They are known for their simplicity and portability. Electrically operated diaphragm pumps, on the other hand, utilize electric motors to drive the diaphragm, offering more precise flow control and efficiency. Both types are widely used in sectors such as pharmaceuticals, food processing, and chemical manufacturing.</p></p>
