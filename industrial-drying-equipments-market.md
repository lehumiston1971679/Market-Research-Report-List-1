<p><p>The industrial drying equipment market encompasses various technologies tailored for efficient moisture removal from materials. Rotary dryers utilize a rotating drum to continuously expose materials to hot air. Dispersion dryers enhance evaporation by promoting surface area exposure using vaporization strategies. Fluid bed dryers suspend particles in a stream of hot air for uniform drying. Belt dryers move materials on a conveyor belt through heated zones, ensuring even drying. Other types include vacuum and microwave dryers, each serving specific applications across industries.</p></p>
