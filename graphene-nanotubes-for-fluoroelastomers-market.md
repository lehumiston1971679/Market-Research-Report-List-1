The market is primarily divided into two types: single-walled nanotubes (SWNTs) and multi-walled nanotubes (MWNTs). SWNTs consist of a single layer of graphene rolled into a tube, offering superior flexibility and conductivity. In contrast, MWNTs contain multiple layers, providing enhanced mechanical strength and thermal stability. Both types improve the performance of fluoroelastomers, making them suitable for demanding applications in various industries, including automotive and aerospace.</p></p>
