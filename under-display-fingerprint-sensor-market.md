<p><p>The under-display fingerprint sensor market encompasses two main types: optical and ultrasonic fingerprint modules. Optical fingerprint modules use light to capture fingerprint images through the display, offering a cost-effective solution, though they may face challenges in low-light conditions and accuracy. Conversely, ultrasonic fingerprint modules utilize sound waves to create a detailed 3D map of the fingerprint, providing enhanced security and performance, particularly in varied environments. Both technologies are crucial for the growing demand for biometric authentication in devices.</p></p>
