<p><ul><li>Magnetic Adjustment</li><li>Gravity Adjustment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The programmable valves market offers two primary types: magnetic adjustment and gravity adjustment valves. Magnetic adjustment valves utilize electromagnetic mechanisms for precise control of flow and pressure, providing flexibility and responsiveness in various applications. In contrast, gravity adjustment valves rely on gravitational forces to regulate fluid movement, ensuring simpler operation and cost efficiency. Both types cater to different industrial needs, allowing for optimized performance in fluid management systems across sectors such as manufacturing, agriculture, and HVAC.</p></p>
