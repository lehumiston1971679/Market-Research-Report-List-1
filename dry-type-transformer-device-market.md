<p><strong>The Dry-Type Transformer Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Core Type</li><li>Shell Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dry-type transformer market is primarily categorized into core type and shell type transformers. Core type transformers have windings wrapped around a core, leading to a compact design and efficient heat dissipation. They are often used in industrial applications. In contrast, shell type transformers feature a core that surrounds the windings, providing better protection and stability, making them ideal for environments where space is limited. Both types are increasingly favored for their safety and low maintenance requirements in various applications.</p></p>
