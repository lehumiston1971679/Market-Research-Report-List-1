<p><p>Hotel Central Reservation Systems (CRS) can be categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises systems. Cloud-based systems offer flexibility and scalability, allowing hotels to manage reservations remotely through the internet, enhancing accessibility and reducing IT costs. Conversely, on-premises systems are installed locally within a hotel’s infrastructure, providing greater control over data security and customization but requiring higher upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. Both types serve to streamline reservation processes and improve operational efficiency.</p></p>
