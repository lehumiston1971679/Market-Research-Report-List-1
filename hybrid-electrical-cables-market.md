<p><p>The hybrid electrical cables market consists of two main types: single-mode and multimode. Single-mode cables are designed for long-distance communication, utilizing a single light path to minimize signal loss and provide higher bandwidth, making them ideal for telecommunications and high-speed networks. In contrast, multimode cables use multiple light paths, allowing for shorter distance transmission and lower costs, commonly used in local area networks (LANs) and data centers. Both types cater to diverse connectivity needs in various applications.</p></p>
