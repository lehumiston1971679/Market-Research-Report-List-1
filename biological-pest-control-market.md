<p><p>The biological pest control market encompasses various types designed to manage pests naturally. Predatory mites and insects target and feed on harmful pests, offering an eco-friendly alternative to chemical pesticides. Nematodes, microscopic roundworms, infect and control pest populations in the soil. Bioinsecticides, derived from natural organisms or their by-products, effectively target specific pests while minimizing harm to beneficial species. Collectively, these biological methods contribute to sustainable agriculture by reducing reliance on synthetic chemicals and promoting healthier ecosystems.</p></p>
