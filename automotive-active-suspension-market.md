<p><p>The automotive active suspension market comprises various technologies designed to enhance vehicle performance and comfort. Air suspension uses air-filled bags to adjust ride height and absorb shocks. Hydraulic suspension employs fluid dynamics for real-time damping adjustments, offering improved stability. Electromagnetic suspension utilizes magnetic forces to control damping, providing responsive handling. Electro-hydraulic suspension combines electric and hydraulic systems for precise control and adaptability, optimizing both ride comfort and handling. Each type caters to diverse vehicle requirements and preferences, enhancing overall driving experiences.</p></p>
