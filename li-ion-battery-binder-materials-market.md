<p><ul><li>Anode Binder</li><li>Cathode Binder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Li-ion battery binder materials market is categorized into anode binders and cathode binders. Anode binders, typically made from materials like polyvinylidene fluoride (PVDF), help to maintain the structural integrity of the anode while enhancing electrode performance. Conversely, cathode binders, often based on similar polymers, serve a critical role in ensuring adhesion between active materials and conductive additives in the cathode. Both types are essential for improving battery efficiency, lifespan, and overall performance in various applications.</p></p>
