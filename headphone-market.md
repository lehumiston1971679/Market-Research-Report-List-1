<p><ul><li>Over-Ear</li><li>On-Ear</li><li>In-Ear</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The headphone market is primarily divided into three types: over-ear, on-ear, and in-ear. Over-ear headphones feature large ear cups that encompass the ears, providing excellent sound isolation and comfort for long listening sessions. On-ear headphones rest directly on the ears, offering a balance between portability and sound quality, though with less isolation. In-ear headphones, or earbuds, are compact and fit directly in the ear canal, making them ideal for on-the-go use, but they may sacrifice some audio depth. Each type caters to different user preferences and lifestyles.</p></p>
