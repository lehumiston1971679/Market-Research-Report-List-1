<p><p>The varifocal lenses market encompasses various types, primarily focusing on freely-varifocal lenses and multifocal lenses. Freely-varifocal lenses offer continuous adjustment of focal length, allowing users to easily transition between different distances, enhancing versatility in vision correction. In contrast, multifocal lenses incorporate multiple optical zones for different viewing distances, typically featuring distinct sections for near, intermediate, and far vision. Both lens types cater to diverse visual needs, providing tailored solutions for individuals requiring flexibility in their sight correction.</p></p>
