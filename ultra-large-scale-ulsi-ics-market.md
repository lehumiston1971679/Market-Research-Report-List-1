<p><p>The Ultra Large Scale Integration (ULSI) IC market is primarily divided into thin film and thick film ICs. Thin film ICs are characterized by layers of semiconductor material deposited on a substrate, offering high compatibility with miniaturization and integration, commonly used in sensors and displays. Thick film ICs, on the other hand, utilize printed conductive and insulating materials, making them suitable for applications requiring higher power handling and durability, such as automotive and industrial electronics. Both types cater to diverse electronic needs and innovations.</p></p>
