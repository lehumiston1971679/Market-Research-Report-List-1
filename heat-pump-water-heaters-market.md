<p><p>Heat pump water heaters are categorized into three main types: air source, water source, and ground source. Air source heat pumps extract heat from the air, making them ideal for moderate climates. Water source heat pumps utilize nearby water bodies to transfer heat, offering efficient heating in specific locations. Ground source heat pumps, or geothermal systems, leverage stable underground temperatures for efficient heating and cooling. Each type serves different needs based on climate, location, and energy efficiency requirements.</p></p>
