<p><p>Hotel preventive maintenance software can be categorized into two main types: on-premises and cloud-based. On-premises software is installed locally on a hotel's servers, providing complete control over data and security but requiring significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. Cloud-based software, on the other hand, is hosted online, allowing for easier access, automatic updates, and scalability while reducing infrastructure costs. Both types aim to streamline maintenance processes, enhance operational efficiency, and improve overall guest experience in hotels.</p></p>
