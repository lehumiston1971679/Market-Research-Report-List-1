<p><strong>The CMOS Image Transducers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FSI Technology</li><li>BSI Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CMOS image transducer market is primarily categorized into two types: Front-Side Illuminated (FSI) and Back-Side Illuminated (BSI) technologies. FSI technology allows light to enter the sensor from the front, which can limit sensitivity and dynamic range. In contrast, BSI technology places the photodiode behind the circuitry, improving light capture efficiency, especially in low-light conditions. This shift towards BSI has led to better image quality and performance in applications ranging from smartphones to industrial imaging, driving market growth.</p></p>
