<p><p>The programmable syringe pump market can be categorized into three main types: single syringe, dual syringes, and multiple syringes. Single syringe pumps are designed to administer one fluid at a controlled rate, ideal for simple applications. Dual syringe pumps allow simultaneous delivery of two different fluids or medications, enhancing versatility in treatment. Multiple syringes systems can handle several syringes, enabling complex infusion protocols for comprehensive patient care, particularly in critical and research settings, where precise dosing is crucial.</p></p>
