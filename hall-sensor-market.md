<p><ul><li>Analog Output Hall Sensor</li><li>Digital Output Hall Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Hall Sensor Market is primarily divided into two types: Analog Output Hall Sensors and Digital Output Hall Sensors. Analog Output Hall Sensors provide a continuous voltage signal that corresponds to the magnetic field strength, allowing for precise measurements in applications like motor control and position sensing. In contrast, Digital Output Hall Sensors produce discrete signals, indicating the presence or absence of a magnetic field, making them suitable for applications like switch detection and speed measurement, offering simplicity and reliability.</p></p>
