<p><p>Contact center markets can be categorized into two main types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise contact centers require businesses to invest in physical infrastructure and manage their operations internally, offering full control over systems and data. In contrast, cloud-based contact centers operate via the internet, allowing companies to access services and resources remotely, often with lower upfront costs and greater scalability. Both types cater to diverse business needs, with on-premise providing customization and cloud-based offering flexibility and ease of deployment.</p></p>
