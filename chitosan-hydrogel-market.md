<p><p>The Chitosan Hydrogel market is primarily categorized into two types: injection type and non-injection type. Injection type hydrogels are designed for direct delivery into tissues or organs, facilitating targeted treatment in medical applications like drug delivery and tissue engineering. In contrast, non-injection type hydrogels are used externally, often in wound dressings and topical applications, providing moisture retention and promoting healing. Both types leverage chitosan's biocompatibility and biodegradability, making them valuable in various biomedical and pharmaceutical sectors.</p></p>
