<p><p>The passive digitizer market is classified into two main types: digital output and analog output. Digital output digitizers convert continuous signals into discrete digital data, enabling precise data processing and integration with digital systems. Conversely, analog output digitizers maintain the signal in its analog form, allowing for real-time monitoring and interaction with analog devices. Both types cater to different applications, with digital output suiting advanced computing needs and analog output being ideal for traditional systems requiring straightforward signal representation.</p></p>
