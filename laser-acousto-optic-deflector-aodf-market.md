<p><p>A Laser Acousto-Optic Deflector (AODF) is a device that utilizes the interaction between laser light and sound waves to deflect or scan laser beams rapidly and precisely. AODFs are widely used in applications such as laser beam steering, fiber-optic communication, and imaging systems.</p><p>The Laser Acousto-Optic Deflector (AODF) Market is witnessing growth due to increasing demand for precise laser systems across various industries, including telecommunications, military, and medical sectors.
