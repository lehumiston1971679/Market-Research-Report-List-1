<p><strong>The Handheld Tachometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Contact</li><li>Contact</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld tachometer market is divided into two main types: contact and non-contact tachometers. Contact tachometers measure the rotational speed of an object by direct physical contact, typically utilizing a mechanical wheel or probe that attaches to the rotating surface. In contrast, non-contact tachometers use laser or infrared technology to detect speed from a distance, offering convenience and minimizing wear on the object being measured. Both types cater to various industries, enabling precise monitoring of rotational speeds.</p></p>
