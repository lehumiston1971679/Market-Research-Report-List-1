<p><ul><li>Closed Die Forging</li><li>Open Die Forging</li><li>Extrusion</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The press forging machine market encompasses various forging techniques. Closed die forging involves shaping materials within two dies to achieve intricate designs and high precision. Open die forging uses flat or simple-shaped dies, allowing for larger workpieces and more basic shapes. Extrusion is a process where materials are forced through a die to create long shapes with uniform cross-sections. Other methods may include drop forging and roll forging, each providing unique advantages for specific manufacturing needs and applications.</p></p>
