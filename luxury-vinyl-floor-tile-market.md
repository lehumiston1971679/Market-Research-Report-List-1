<p><p>The Luxury Vinyl Floor Tile (LVT) market is primarily divided into two types: Flexible LVT and Rigid LVT. Flexible LVT is characterized by its thinner, pliable composition, allowing for easy installation and adaptability to uneven surfaces. It's often used in residential settings. In contrast, Rigid LVT features a thicker, more stable core, providing enhanced durability and resistance to moisture, making it suitable for commercial applications. Both types offer a range of designs that mimic natural materials, combining aesthetics with practicality.</p></p>
