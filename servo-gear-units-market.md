<p><p>The servo gear units market comprises various types, notably linear servo gearboxes and right angle servo gearboxes. Linear servo gearboxes convert rotational motion into linear movement, providing high precision in applications like robotics and automation. In contrast, right angle servo gearboxes feature a compact design that changes the direction of motion by 90 degrees, making them ideal for space-constrained environments. Both types enhance performance, efficiency, and durability, catering to diverse industrial applications across the manufacturing and automation sectors.</p></p>
