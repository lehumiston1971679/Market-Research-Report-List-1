<p><p>The wood-based activated carbon market is primarily categorized into three types: powdered activated carbon (PAC), granular activated carbon (GAC), and others. PAC has a fine particle size, making it suitable for applications requiring quick adsorption, such as air purification and water treatment. GAC features larger granules, offering lower pressure drop and better flow rates, ideal for industrial processes and drinking water filtration. The "others" category includes specialized forms like extruded and pelletized activated carbon, catering to niche applications while enhancing overall market diversity.</p></p>
