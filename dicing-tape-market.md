<p><p>The Dicing Tape market is categorized into two primary types: UV Curable and Non-UV Types. UV Curable Dicing Tape is designed to bond effectively with wafers and can be cured quickly using UV light, offering benefits like enhanced adhesion and easy removal. In contrast, Non-UV Dicing Tape relies on heat or pressure for curing, providing versatility in various applications. Both types are essential in semiconductor manufacturing and precision cutting, catering to different operational requirements and preferences in the industry.</p></p>
