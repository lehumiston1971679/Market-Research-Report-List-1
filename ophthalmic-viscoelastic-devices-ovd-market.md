<p><p>Ophthalmic Viscoelastic Devices (OVDs) are crucial in ophthalmic surgery, particularly cataract extraction and intraocular lens implantation. The market consists of dispersive OVDs, which provide high viscoelasticity to protect tissues; cohesive OVDs, offering strong interfacial adhesion for easier removal; combination OVDs, which blend properties of both for versatility; and visco-adaptive OVDs, designed to adapt their viscosity based on the surgical environment. Each type serves specific surgical needs, enhancing safety and outcomes in ophthalmic procedures.</p></p>
