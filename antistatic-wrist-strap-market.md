<p><p>The antistatic wrist strap market is primarily categorized into corded and cordless wrist straps. Corded wrist straps are connected to a grounding point via a cable, providing a direct pathway for electric discharge, making them reliable for constant protection in static-sensitive environments. Both types serve to mitigate static electricity, ensuring safe handling of electronic components and equipment.</p></p>
