Light Weight recycled concrete uses porous or lightweight aggregates, reducing overall weight while maintaining adequate strength, suitable for non-load-bearing walls, insulation, and lightweight construction. Both types promote sustainability by reusing concrete waste, reducing landfill dependency, and conserving natural resources, catering to diverse construction needs.</p></p>
