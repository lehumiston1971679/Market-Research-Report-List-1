<p><p>The GaAs LPE (Liquid Phase Epitaxy) epitaxial wafers market is segmented into N-type and P-type layers. N-type layers are doped with elements like silicon or sulfur, resulting in a surplus of electrons, making them suitable for high-speed electronic applications. In contrast, P-type layers are doped with elements like Zinc or Magnesium, creating 'holes' or positive charge carriers, ideal for optoelectronic devices. Both layers are crucial for fabricating advanced semiconductor devices, including lasers and photodetectors, enhancing overall device performance.</p></p>
