<p><p>The copper foil market with thickness greater than 70 μm primarily includes two types: electrolytic copper foil and rolled copper foil. Electrolytic copper foil is produced through an electrochemical process, offering excellent conductivity and uniform thickness, making it suitable for high-performance applications like printed circuit boards (PCBs). In contrast, rolled copper foil is manufactured by mechanically rolling copper sheets, delivering higher strength and durability, often used in applications requiring enhanced thermal and electrical properties, such as in battery technologies and electromagnetic shielding.</p></p>
