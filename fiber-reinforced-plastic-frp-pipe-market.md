<p><p>Fiber Reinforced Plastic (FRP) pipes are categorized into three main types: Glass Reinforced Plastic (GRP), Glass Reinforced Epoxy (GRE), and Glass Reinforced Vinylester (GRV). GRP pipes, made from glass fibers and polyester resin, are widely used for water and wastewater applications due to their corrosion resistance and lightweight properties. GRE pipes utilize epoxy resin, offering superior strength and chemical resistance, making them suitable for industrial applications. GRV pipes, combining glass fibers with vinylester resin, provide enhanced durability and thermal resistance, ideal for harsh environments.</p></p>
