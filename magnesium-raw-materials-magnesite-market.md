<p><p>The magnesium raw materials market, particularly for magnesite, is categorized into two primary types: phanerocrystalline magnesite and cryptocrystalline magnesite. Phanerocrystalline magnesite features a coarse crystalline structure, offering higher purity and is typically used in high-performance applications, such as refractory materials and specialized ceramics. In contrast, cryptocrystalline magnesite has a fine-grained, dense structure, making it suitable for less demanding applications, including construction and agriculture. Both types play crucial roles in various industries, reflecting their unique properties and applications.</p></p>
