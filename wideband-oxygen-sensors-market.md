<p><ul><li>Battery Type</li><li>Critical Current Type</li><li>Pump Battery Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Wideband Oxygen Sensors market includes three main types: Battery Type, Critical Current Type, and Pump Battery Type. Battery Type sensors use an integrated battery to power the sensor, offering simplicity and reliability. Critical Current Type sensors measure oxygen levels by analyzing the current required to maintain a specific oxygen concentration, ensuring precise readings. Pump Battery Type sensors combine a pump cell and a battery, enabling accurate and stable oxygen measurement, particularly in dynamic conditions. Each type caters to specific automotive and industrial applications, balancing performance and cost.</p></p>
