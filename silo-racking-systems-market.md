<p><p>Silo racking systems are designed to efficiently store and manage inventory. Single deep systems allow pallets to be stored directly in single rows, providing easy access but limited storage density. Double deep systems enhance storage efficiency by placing two pallets deep, requiring special forklifts for access but maximizing space utilization. Other types include drive-in racking, which allows for high-density storage with fewer aisles, and mobile racking, which optimizes space and accessibility by moving racks on tracks.</p></p>
