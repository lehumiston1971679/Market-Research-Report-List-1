<p><p>Smart Label Materials encompass a range of adhesive products used for labeling, which can be categorized mainly into solvent-based and solvent-free/water-based types. Solvent-based products typically utilize organic solvents to enhance adhesion and durability, making them suitable for various surfaces. In contrast, solvent-free or water-based products prioritize environmental safety and reduced VOC emissions, often using water as the primary carrier. Both types cater to different applications, from industrial labeling to consumer goods, while balancing performance and sustainability.</p></p>
