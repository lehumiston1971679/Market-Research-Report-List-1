<p><ul><li>Vendor Neutral Archive System(VNA)</li><li>Picture Archiving and Communications System (PACS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical Imaging Management Market encompasses two primary systems: Vendor Neutral Archive (VNA) and Picture Archiving and Communications System (PACS). VNA serves as a centralized data repository, allowing healthcare providers to store, retrieve, and share imaging data from various sources irrespective of the vendor. In contrast, PACS focuses on acquiring, storing, and managing images and related data, providing clinicians with easy access for diagnosis and treatment. Together, they enhance interoperability, streamline workflows, and improve patient care in medical imaging.</p></p>
