<p><strong>The Marine Seismic Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sub-Bottom Profilers</li><li>Seismic Sensors</li><li>Scalar Sensor</li><li>Vector Sensor</li><li>Geophones & Hydrophones</li><li>Streamers</li><li>Single Streamer</li><li>Multiple Streamers</li><li>Air / Water Guns</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The marine seismic equipment market encompasses various technologies for subsurface exploration. Sub-bottom profilers provide detailed imagery of sediment layers. Seismic sensors, including scalar and vector types, measure pressure changes and motion in the water. Geophones and hydrophones detect seismic waves on land and underwater, respectively. Streamers, available as single or multiple units, are towed behind vessels to gather seismic data. Air/water guns generate controlled acoustic pulses for imaging the seafloor and subsurface structures, ensuring efficient geological mapping.</p></p>
