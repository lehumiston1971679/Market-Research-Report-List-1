<p><p>The Electric Vertical Take-Off and Landing (eVTOL) manned vehicle market comprises three primary types: Tilt-X, Lift+Cruise, and Multi-copters. Tilt-X vehicles utilize rotor systems that tilt to transition between vertical takeoff and horizontal flight, offering efficiency and speed. Lift+Cruise designs separate lift and forward propulsion, often featuring fixed wings and rotors for enhanced range. Multi-copters rely on multiple rotors for lift and stability, making them versatile and easier to control, ideal for urban air mobility applications. Each type caters to varying operational needs and efficiencies.</p></p>
