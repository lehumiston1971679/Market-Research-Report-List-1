<p><ul><li>Squeezing Method</li><li>Leaching Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flaxseed oil is extracted primarily through two methods: squeezing and leaching. The squeezing method, also known as cold pressing, involves mechanically pressing flaxseeds to release oil while retaining nutrients and flavor. This method typically produces high-quality oil with a rich taste. In contrast, the leaching method involves using solvents to extract oil from seeds, often resulting in a higher yield but potentially compromising the oil's quality and nutritional value. Both methods cater to different consumer preferences and market demands.</p></p>
