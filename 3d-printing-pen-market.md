<p><p>The 3D printing pen market is primarily segmented into two types: Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM) and Stereolithography (SLA). FDM 3D printing pens extrude thermoplastic filament, allowing users to create solid structures layer by layer, making them popular for DIY projects and education. Conversely, SLA 3D printing pens utilize a light source to cure liquid resin, producing highly detailed and smooth finishes, ideal for intricate designs and artistic applications. Each type caters to specific user needs and preferences in the creative space.</p></p>
