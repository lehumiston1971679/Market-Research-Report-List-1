<p><strong>The Semiconductor MEMS Probe Cards Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vertical MEMS Probe Cards</li><li>Cantilever MEMS Probe Cards</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor MEMS probe cards market is primarily divided into vertical MEMS probe cards and cantilever MEMS probe cards. Vertical MEMS probe cards utilize a vertical alignment of probes, enabling high-density contact with the device under test, which enhances performance in applications like advanced packaging. On the other hand, cantilever MEMS probe cards employ flexible, cantilevered probes that can adapt to various surfaces, offering greater versatility for testing larger or irregularly shaped devices, but may have limitations in density compared to vertical designs.</p></p>
