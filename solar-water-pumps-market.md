<p><p>The solar water pumps market features several types catering to diverse needs. DC Surface Suction pumps are ideal for shallow water sources, using direct current for efficient operation. AC Submersible pumps are designed for deeper water extraction, powered by alternating current, making them suitable for various applications. DC Submersible pumps are highly efficient for deep wells and remote locations, working on solar energy. AC Surface pumps, while also utilizing AC power, are positioned above the water source, ideal for lower depth extractions.</p></p>
