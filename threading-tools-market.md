<p><p>The threading tools market is divided into external and internal types based on their application. External threading tools are designed for creating threads on the outer surfaces of cylindrical objects, commonly used in applications like bolts and screws. In contrast, internal threading tools are utilized for forming threads inside holes or tubes, essential for parts like nuts and fittings. Each type serves distinct manufacturing needs, catering to various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and general engineering.</p></p>
