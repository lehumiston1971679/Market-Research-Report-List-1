<p><p>The medical balloon system market encompasses various types tailored to specific clinical needs. The Quick Exchange Type allows for rapid balloon exchange during procedures, enhancing efficiency. The Overall Exchange Type facilitates complete exchange of devices without disturbing the guide wire, while the Fixed Guide Wire Type offers stability and ease of use in complex interventions. Lastly, the Perfusion Type enables fluid delivery during balloon inflation, supporting vascular procedures by maintaining blood flow and improving outcomes. Each type addresses unique procedural challenges in medical practice.</p></p>
