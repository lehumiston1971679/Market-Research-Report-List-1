<p><p>The PU foam sealant market is primarily divided into two types: one-component and two-component systems. </p><p>One-component sealants are pre-packaged and ready to use, expanding upon exposure to moisture in the air. They're popular for their ease of application and convenience. </p><p>In contrast, two-component sealants require the mixing of two separate components before use, resulting in superior durability and strength. This type is often preferred for demanding applications, providing robust insulation and sealing performance in construction and industrial settings.</p></p>
