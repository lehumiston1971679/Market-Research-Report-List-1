<p><p>The Microwave Radiometer market is categorized into two main types: Unpolarised and Dual Polarised. Unpolarised radiometers measure the total radiation without distinguishing between polarizations, making them simpler and often more cost-effective for applications like atmospheric monitoring. Conversely, Dual Polarised radiometers capture information in two orthogonal polarization states, providing greater detail and accuracy in data, which is essential for applications like weather forecasting, environmental monitoring, and remote sensing. This differentiation enables tailored solutions for various scientific and industrial needs.</p></p>
