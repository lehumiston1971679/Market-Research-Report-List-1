<p><p>The ducted fume hoods market is categorized into two primary types based on materials: antistatic and non-antistatic. Antistatic materials are designed to prevent static electricity buildup, making them ideal for environments dealing with flammable or sensitive chemicals, ensuring safety and reducing hazards. Non-antistatic materials, while effective for general laboratory use, may not offer the same level of protection against static charges. The choice between these materials impacts safety, performance, and suitability for specific laboratory applications.</p></p>
