<p><p>TMR (Tunneling Magnetoresistance) Sensing ICs are classified into two main types: Analog and Digital. Analog TMR Sensing ICs provide continuous output signals proportional to magnetic fields, making them suitable for applications like position sensing and current detection. In contrast, Digital TMR Sensing ICs deliver discrete output signals, often featuring built-in processing capabilities, ideal for applications requiring precise digital interfacing, such as in automotive and industrial systems. Both types cater to diverse demands in the sensing technology market.</p></p>
