<p><p>The Metal Oxide Varistor (MOV) market is primarily divided into two types: SMD (Surface Mount Device) and Disc type MOVs. SMD MOVs are designed for compact electronic devices, offering space-saving benefits and ease of integration on circuit boards. In contrast, Disc type MOVs are larger and used in applications requiring higher voltage handling and energy absorption, such as power supply protection. Both types play critical roles in safeguarding electronic equipment from voltage surges and transient overvoltages.</p></p>
