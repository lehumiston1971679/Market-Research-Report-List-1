<p><ul><li>Solution Polymerization</li><li>Suspension Polymerization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The EP Copolymer market comprises two main types based on the polymerization method: solution polymerization and suspension polymerization. In solution polymerization, the monomers are dissolved in a solvent, leading to better control over molecular weight and polymer characteristics. Conversely, suspension polymerization involves dispersing monomers in a liquid medium, allowing for easier handling and a more stable final product. Both methods cater to various industrial applications, influencing properties like viscosity, clarity, and mechanical strength.</p></p>
