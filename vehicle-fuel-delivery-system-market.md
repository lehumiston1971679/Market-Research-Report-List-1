<p><strong>The Vehicle Fuel Delivery System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pump-Line-Nozzle Delivery Systems</li><li>Common Rail Delivery Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vehicle fuel delivery system market is primarily divided into two types: Pump-Line-Nozzle (PLN) delivery systems and Common Rail delivery systems. PLN systems involve a pump delivering fuel through a line to injectors, suitable for conventional engines. In contrast, Common Rail systems store high-pressure fuel in a common rail, allowing multiple injectors to draw fuel as needed, enhancing efficiency and performance. Both systems play critical roles in optimizing fuel delivery, emissions control, and engine performance in modern vehicles.</p></p>
