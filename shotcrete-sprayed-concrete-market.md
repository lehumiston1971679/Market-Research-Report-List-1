<p><ul><li>Dry Mix</li><li>Wet Mix</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Shotcrete and sprayed concrete markets are categorized into two primary types: dry mix and wet mix. Dry mix shotcrete involves dry materials conveyed through a hose, where water is added at the nozzle before application, allowing for better control over the water content and faster application. In contrast, wet mix shotcrete consists of pre-mixed wet materials that are pumped to the spraying nozzle, providing a smoother finish. Both processes are utilized in construction for their durability and versatility in various applications.</p></p>
