<p><p>The rugged thermal cameras market is segmented into three types based on their wavelength sensitivity. Short Wavelength Infrared (SWIR) cameras are optimized for detecting subtle temperature variations and are effective in low-light conditions. Mid-Wavelength Infrared (MWIR) cameras excel in industrial and military applications, offering enhanced sensitivity and resolution for detecting heat patterns. Long-Wavelength Infrared (LWIR) cameras, commonly used for surveillance and environmental monitoring, provide excellent thermal imaging capabilities, detecting heat signatures from a distance regardless of weather conditions.</p></p>
