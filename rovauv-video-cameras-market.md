<p><p>The ROV/AUV video camera market is primarily categorized into two sensor types: CMOS and CCD. CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) sensors offer lower power consumption and faster processing speeds, making them ideal for real-time video applications in underwater robotics. In contrast, CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) sensors typically deliver superior image quality and higher sensitivity in low light conditions, making them suitable for detailed underwater exploration. Both sensor types cater to different operational requirements, impacting their adoption in various marine applications.</p></p>
