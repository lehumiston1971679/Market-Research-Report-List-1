<p><p>The AGV driverless forklift market is categorized into three main navigation types: Laser Navigation, Magnetic Navigation, and Visual Navigation. Laser Navigation uses lasers to map environments, providing high precision and flexibility in dynamic settings. Magnetic Navigation relies on pre-installed magnetic tapes or strips, offering a cost-effective solution with reliable path following but limited flexibility. Visual Navigation employs cameras and computer vision to interpret surroundings, enabling advanced obstacle detection and adaptability, making it suitable for complex environments. Each type serves unique operational needs.</p></p>
