<p><p>Diamond-coated cutting tools are advanced tools used for precision machining, offering superior hardness, wear resistance, and thermal conductivity. The market is segmented into **CVD Diamond-coated** and **PVD Diamond-coated** tools. **CVD Diamond-coated** tools are created using Chemical Vapor Deposition, forming a thick, durable diamond layer ideal for high-performance applications like machining composites and ceramics. **PVD Diamond-coated** tools use Physical Vapor Deposition, producing a thinner, smoother coating suitable for fine finishing and precision cutting of softer materials like aluminum and non-ferrous metals. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
