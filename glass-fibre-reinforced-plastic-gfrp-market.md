<p><p>Glass Fibre Reinforced Plastic (GFRP) is a composite material made from a polymer matrix reinforced with glass fibers, offering high strength and lightweight properties. In the market, GFRP is categorized into thermosetting and thermoplastic products. Thermosetting plastic products are permanently hard and cannot be re-molded, making them ideal for durable applications like automotive and aerospace components. Conversely, thermoplastic plastic products can be re-melted and reshaped, providing flexibility for manufacturing processes and applications in consumer goods and construction.</p></p>
