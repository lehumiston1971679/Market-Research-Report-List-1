<p><strong>The Silicon-Based Fingerprint Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Swipe Sensors</li><li>Area Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The silicon-based fingerprint sensors market is primarily categorized into swipe sensors and area sensors. Swipe sensors capture fingerprints by sliding a finger across the sensor surface, offering compact designs often used in mobile devices and access control systems. Area sensors, on the other hand, scan an entire fingerprint at once, providing higher accuracy and detail, making them suitable for applications like secure identification and law enforcement. Both types leverage silicon technology for enhanced performance and robustness in various applications.</p></p>
