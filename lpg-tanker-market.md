<p><p>The LPG tanker market comprises various vessel types based on size and capacity. **VLGCs (Very Large Gas Carriers)** are the largest, typically transporting 80,000-85,000 cubic meters of LPG, primarily used for long-haul international trade. **LGCs (Large Gas Carriers)** are mid-sized, carrying 35,000-50,000 cubic meters, suitable for regional routes. **MGCs (Medium Gas Carriers)** handle 12,000-35,000 cubic meters, ideal for shorter distances. **SGCs (Small Gas Carriers)** are the smallest, with capacities below 12,000 cubic meters, often used for coastal or domestic distribution. Each type serves specific market needs, balancing cargo volume and operational flexibility.</p></p>
