<p><p>Continuous Glucose Monitoring (CGM) systems are categorized into two primary market types: wearable and implantable. Wearable CGMs are external devices that attach to the skin, providing real-time glucose readings and convenience for users. In contrast, implantable CGMs are surgically placed under the skin, offering long-term glucose monitoring with reduced need for frequent replacements. Both types enhance diabetes management by delivering continuous data, enabling users to make informed decisions about their glucose levels and overall health.</p></p>
